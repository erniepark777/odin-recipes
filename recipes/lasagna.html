Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • Meat - italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce and tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spice and seasoning - parsley, basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese - parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasanga according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
